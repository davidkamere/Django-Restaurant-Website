BEHIND THE NAME :

When I think of Mama, the first thing that comes to mind is my mother. I wanted the name to be centered around the rich African culture. I thought about the things my mom does that scream “Typical African Mum” then it hit me.

Perhaps my best memory in this respect is of when we attended weddings as a family. We had to sit through the infamously long summons waiting for the moment when everyone would head to the reception. Where the cream of all African weddings is….The Food.

Fortunately, we didn't have to queue with the uncultured folk that stood in line on behalf of ten other people that were seated in their tents waiting for their "designated queue person” to get to the front of the buffet then magically show up. I must admit though, I did admire their organization and coordination. I always wondered if they had meetings before these weddings.

Regardless, we never had to worry about that, because our mum was the plug. She wasn't afraid to get her hands a little dirty to keep us fed and in good time. What an angel. We got our food brought to us where we were seated. That plate was sentimental and the family was happy . So as cheesy as it may sound, that’s how I ended up with the name Mama’s Plate
