ABOUT MAMA :

Hello, Everyone

My name is Getrude Sharon Ayieko, popularly known as Mama. I’m a 24 year old self-taught chef. In 2018, I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in financial engineering from JKUAT. Goes to say that I really love math.

I learnt all I know about the kitchen from personal experiences ranging from my own curiosity or the love of different flavor combinations. Basic cooking skills were acquired from my mother and I am also a commited student of youtube.

I started Mama’s plate back in university in 2016 where I’d just cook for friends and family occasionally then after a constant push from my close friends to put my content out there, I started the instagram page in 2017 September 6th with this as my first post

BEHIND THE NAME :

When I think of Mama, the first thing that comes to mind is my mother. Since I wanted the backbone of the name to be centered around the African culture. I sat and thought about the things my mum does that are so “African Mum” then it hit me.

There we are at a wedding with the whole family and we’ve sat through the long summons and barely understood anything then everyone heads to the reception right after and we all know what the main occasion of African weddings is….The Food.

We never really had to stand in the long lines filled with people that have stood in line on behalf of ten other people that were seated in the tents timing when their “person” gets to the front of the buffet then magically show up pushing the next person ten steps behind.

We never had to worry about that, because our mum was the plug. We got our food brought to us where we were seated. That plate that we always got held a sentimental meaning to all of us in the family. So as cheesy as it may sound, that’s how I ended up with the name Mama’s plate
